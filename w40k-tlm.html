Warhammer 40K: The Last Marine

March / 4 / 2025

Warhammer 40,000: The Last Marine is a playable full-game developed over the course of four months as a university project between 39 developers at CITM, using our own custom engine Coffee Engine.

As the Main Enemy Programmer, I was responsible for designing and implementing the entire enemy system — from the base architecture shared by all enemy types, to the full development of the Final Boss fight. I also led coordination within the enemy team, organizing tasks and supporting teammates throughout the development process.

My contributions also extended to audio and VFX integration for enemies, as well as collaborating with other departments and assisting with final preparations for every release.

This project has been one of the most rewarding and challenging experiences of my career so far. I'm incredibly proud of what our team accomplished — and I'm excited for the opportunities and challenges ahead.
